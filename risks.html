Risks of Bitcoin

Key Risks

  • Volatility: Bitcoin prices can fluctuate dramatically, leading to potential losses.
  • Regulatory Risks: Governments may impose regulations that could affect the use and value of Bitcoin.
  • Security Risks: Wallets can be hacked, leading to the loss of funds.
  • Lack of Consumer Protection: Transactions are irreversible, and there is limited recourse for fraud.
  • Market Manipulation: The market can be influenced by speculative trading, leading to price manipulation.
